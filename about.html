Sprout & Grow: Your premier organic nursery in Raleigh, North Carolina.

Who We Are:

Sprout & Grow is Raleigh, NC's premier destination for high-quality organic plants, dedicated to serving gardeners who prioritize beautiful, healthy, and sustainable growth. As a greenhouse nursery rooted in transparency and environmental stewardship, we offer a diverse selection of meticulously nurtured plants designed to help you cultivate a thriving garden. Whether you are creating a lush urban oasis in a limited space or converting a backyard into organic beds, our curated collections and eco-friendly approach ensure you can choose confidently and grow successfully.

Beyond providing exceptional greenery, we are committed to empowering our community through expert guidance and educational resources. We aim to inspire home gardeners, environmental enthusiasts, and educators alike by offering practical plant-care tips, hands-on workshops, and seasonal events that foster a deeper connection with nature. We invite you to explore our online catalog or visit us to experience our commitment to integrity and sustainability firsthand, finding the perfect solutions that fit your life and values.

Our Values:

  • Organic Growth: We prioritize the health and vitality of our plants through sustainable and organic practices.
  • Community Engagement: We actively support and participate in local environmental and gardening initiatives.
  • Environmental Stewardship: We are committed to reducing our ecological footprint and promoting eco-friendly gardening methods.
